GUITAR LESSONS

Learning to play the guitar shouldn’t be complicated.

In my lessons, I like to focus on what your goals are and then develop a learning plan that helps you to achieve these. I understand that everyone has their own individual goals for learning, so I work hard to ensure that the material suits the student.

I teach all ages, styles, and abilities, and I have a lot of experience teaching a broad range of different students. Whether you want to learn to strum a few new chords, master a tricky guitar solo, understand music theory better, or anything else, I can help you bring your playing to the next level.

ABOUT

I am an Australian guitarist now living in Berlin. I have been playing guitar for over 15 years and have a large amount of experience playing in various different bands and ensembles. I began learning guitar while I was still in high school and during this time, I developed a strong passion for music. After finishing high school, I moved to Melbourne where I studied at the prestigious Victorian College of the Arts (VCA)/Melbourne University. There I completed a Bachelor of Music (Hons) specialising in ‘Jazz & Improvisation’.

I have worked professionally as a guitarist for the past ten years both teaching and performing. In 2018 I moved to Berlin to continue my musical journey and explore new possibilities. Here I continue to teach and perform regularly in various different groups.
